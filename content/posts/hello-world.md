## Hey, I'm Mukhthar

I just graduated with an Electrical & Electronics Engineering degree from
[TKM College of Engineering](https://tkmce.ac.in) and started my career as a
**QA engineer at [Mitsogo Inc](https://mitsogo.com)**. I'm based in Kochi.

Most of my time outside work goes into two things: **building apps** and
learning everything I can about **agentic AI** — LLMs that don't just answer,
but plan, call tools, and carry a task to completion.

## What this blog will be

A public notebook, basically. Expect posts on:

- **QA notes** — things I learn on the job about test design, automation, and shipping quality software.
- **Build logs** — side projects as I build them, including the rough parts.
- **Agentic AI** — experiments with LLMs, tools, and whatever frameworks I'm poking at.
- **From EEE to software** — the transition, mental models, what transfers and what doesn't.

## Why write in public

Two reasons: it forces me to think clearly, and it leaves a trail so I can look
back six months from now and see how much I've grown. If any of it is useful to
you — even better.

Thanks for stopping by. More soon.
